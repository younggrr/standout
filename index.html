Services

Workshops

I will be running periodic workshops on various aspects of interviewing. Some of the different workshops will include:

Mastering an Interview

This workshop will focus on showing the candidate what is actually happening on the other side of the table.  Learn how to predict the interview questions that will be asked and how best to answer them.  In addition, find out how interviewers make a decision within the first 90 seconds along with more tips and tricks.  

Introduction to Interviews

This workshop is for those who are entering into the workforce for the first time and have little to no experience with interviews.  They say interviews take practice and I couldn’t agree more with this statement.  This workshop will give you a head start in helping you prepare and knowing what to expect from the first phone call scheduling your interview to your job offer! 

Recruiting for Small Businesses

This workshop is geared towards small businesses who would like to brush up on their recruitment skills in order to get the best employee.  Learn the steps that you should take to prepare for an interview, find out what questions you should ask your candidates based on the position you wish to fill and how to decipher the candidates responses. 

Resume and Cover Letter Workshop

Learn how to write an effective cover letter and resume.  Find out from first hand experience how a recruiter will review your application and how to best use this to your advantage

All scheduled workshops can be found on the Events Calendar.  If a specific workshop is not scheduled and you are interested in one, contact me to get your name added to the list and one will be booked!  If you would like to schedule a private workshop for a group of people, please contact me directly and one can be arranged!

1 on 1 Workshops

Workshops are great, but they are not geared towards individual people or businesses.  I am available for 1 on 1 coaching sessions for whatever interview needs you may have.  If you need help answering more detailed questions, you have a more complex recruitment process or you just don’t understand why you keep getting turned down for a position, this is a better option for you.  Please contact me for more information on how we can get started today!

Recruitments

As a small business owner, you may not have the time to spare or the skills to do a proper recruitment to get the best candidates.  I want to help you in any aspect of the recruitment you may need.  Contact me for more information. 

About

I first moved to Peterborough in 1992 with my family, and although I went to Guelph for 6 years for school, I have always referred to Peterborough as my home.  I went to high school at PCVS and then went to the University of Guelph where I received my Bachelor of Commerce, with a Major in Human Resources Management. 

After graduating, I worked in Human Resources for a few manufacturing companies located in the Guelph area that focused on injection molding for the auto industry.  In my roles there, I conducted many interviews from everything between entry level positions and Management.

While being in Guelph, I got married to my amazing husband, Greg, and about a year after that I was expecting my first child, Heather.  It was decided that although Guelph was a great place to live, we wanted to go back home to Peterborough where our families were. 

A couple of kids later and now living in Warsaw, Ontario, I currently work for the County of Peterborough in their Human Resources Department where I routinely conduct recruitments for a variety of different positions and levels. 

My passion for starting up my own coaching company stems from witnessing countless interviews where the candidate had halted any chance they might have in getting the job because of something they did (or didn’t do) in the interview.  Too many times after an interview I have said to myself “if they only…”.  Now I have the chance to help those people! 

I am also excited about the ability to help out local small businesses who do not have the expertise or the manpower in conducting interviews.  It is not as simple as getting a resume and asking candidate a few questions.  Do you know what constitute an illegal question to ask in an interview?  I am here to help!

When I am not working, you will often find me with my husband, Greg and my three children, Heather, Connor and Xander.  In the summer I am often spending a lot of time at my family cottage located in Gananoque, On.  In the winter, I try to spend a lot of time hunkered down under a blanket reading a good book! 

I cannot wait to help you get the career (or the employee) you have always wanted by supporting you through the dreaded INTERVIEW!
